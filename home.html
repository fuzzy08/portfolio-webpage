I'm currently a student at ECPI University, and will be graduating with a Bachelor's in Software Development this fall. Programming is my passion. I absolutley love to learn how computers work, and I especially enjoy finding ways in which to improve a given situation with programming.

Most of my programming experience is in JavaScript, C/C++, and Python. I'm interested in mastering Python to optimize my workflow in my Linux environment and to learn Data Science fundamentals. I would love to one day be able to effectively harvest data and produce accurate and relevant conclusions through the use of Data Science tools and frameworks.

Many projects I'm involved in are affiliated with the ECPI Software Development Club, of which I am Vice President. Some projects include VR with the Oculus Rift, a text-based adventure in C++, a motion-activated light-up board with an arduino, to list a few.
